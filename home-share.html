Okanagan Inclusion is recruiting Contracted Caregivers to provide home sharing support to adults with developmental disabilities.

We are currently seeking individuals or families to share their home and support individuals as needed with the following:

  • accessing community,
  • transportation,
  • personal care,
  • medication management,
  • health care,
  • social,
  • vocational,
  • recreational activities,
  • and more.

Experience in the field is preferred however we will consider applicants without who are willing to learn and have the skill set to become an excellent caregiver.
A complete screening process is completed prior to approval, this includes a criminal record check, driver's abstract, application package, references, etc.
For more information contact:
Michelle Fritsche, Shared Living Coordinator
Phone: (236) 422-0055
Email: michelleb.okinclusion@shaw.ca
